Eric's first Pride.

Eric has stood in a lot of big rooms. He has never stood on a Pride parade route. This is the one. His first, in Chicago, with Crystal beside him and Gordon beside them both.

Gordon said it plainly: of everything on the calendar this year, June 28 is the day he is holding closest of all. The boys will remember the floats. The grown-ups will remember this.

The thread home

All Roads Lead Home.

Here is the quiet magic of Saturday. Everything the boys touch in Chicago, they already build at home in Gordtopia, the Minecraft world Uncle G charts with James, Henry, and Oscar week after week. The trip and the world rhyme.

EXP Rosemont · −7673, 71, 460

Saturday, they rescue a village.

Villager Rescue ends one way: a village falls to a zombie siege, and you brew a Potion of Weakness, throw it with a golden apple, and cure the villagers. That exact cure is already running in Gordtopia, where the boys keep a villager trading hall, an iron golem on watch, and a Nether portal half-built. They will walk through the fiction in Rosemont, then come home and finish building it for real.

Field Museum · SUE, FMNH PR 2081

Saturday, they meet life pulled from stone.

A cast of SUE the T. rex stands across from life-size Tyrantrum. Nearby, Aerodactyl, a creature brought back from DNA locked in ancient amber. The whole hall is one idea: that something can be reconstructed from the smallest trace it leaves behind, and live again. Wonder the boys will carry out the door.
