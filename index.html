The heart of the weekend

Eric's first Pride.

Eric has stood in a lot of big rooms. He has never stood on a Pride parade route. This is the one. His first, in Chicago, with Crystal beside him and Gordon beside them both.

Gordon said it plainly: of everything on the calendar this year, June 28 is the day he is holding closest of all. The boys will remember the floats. The grown-ups will remember this.

The weekend at a glance

Three days in Chicago.

Fri
Jun 26
Arrivals. Gordon's United flight UA580 lands at ORD at 11:25 AM. The Francksens arrive from Wisconsin. Welcome dinner together. Reunion energy.
Sat
Jun 27
The family adventure day. Morning: Pokémon Fossil Museum at the Field Museum (first time outside Japan). Afternoon: Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue at EXP Rosemont (eight interactive rooms, ~55 min). Evening: Northalsted dinner. See the weekend page.
Sun
Jun 28
Chicago Pride Parade. 55th annual. Step-off 11 AM at Sheridan and Broadway. Theme: "Free to Be Proud." Two-mile route through Northalsted. Grand marshals include Tracy Baim, Mona Noriega, Evette Cardona, and the Alliance of Illinois Judges (led by Judge Jill Rose Quinn, Illinois's first openly trans judge).

First time outside Japan. Real fossils next to life-size models of Tyrantrum, Archeops, and friends. SUE the T. rex meets the Pokédex. May 22 2026 – April 11 2027.
