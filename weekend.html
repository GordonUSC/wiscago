Saturday June 27

The family adventure day.

Two of the biggest pop-culture experiences in the country, both in Chicago this summer, both happening for us in one Saturday.

Morning · Field Museum

Pokémon Fossil Museum. The first time this exhibition has traveled outside Japan. Life-size models of fossil Pokémon (Tyrantrum, Archeops, more) displayed alongside real fossils from the Field's collection, including SUE the T. rex. May 22 2026 through April 11 2027.

Tickets

Weekday: $18 adult, $13 kid. Weekend: $20 adult, $14 kid. (Saturday is a weekend day.)

What to see first

SUE the T. rex is in the same building. Make sure the boys get to stand under her.

Programming

The Field is running paleontology activities tied to the exhibit. Check the day-of schedule.

Afternoon · Rosemont

Minecraft Experience: Villager Rescue. An immersive, walk-through Minecraft adventure. Eight interactive rooms, about 55 minutes inside. You and the family gather ingredients, craft a life-saving potion, and rescue the villagers from a zombie attack. All ages welcome.

Where

EXP Rosemont, 9900 Berwyn Ave, Rosemont, IL 60018. Free parking on site.

Tickets

Starting at $28. Multi-pack and group options available.

After the rescue

Trading Post with themed souvenirs. Unlock a commemorative in-game cape with admission.

Evening: dinner in Northalsted (the city's historic LGBTQ+ neighborhood). Easy stroll along the Legacy Walk before settling in for the night.

Sunday June 28

The 55th annual Chicago Pride Parade.

Theme: Free to Be Proud. Stepping off 11 AM at Sheridan and Broadway. Two miles through Northalsted.

The route

South on Broadway → south on Halsted → east on Belmont → south on Broadway → east on Diversey to Cannon Drive. Picks up the energy through the heart of the LGBTQ+ corridor and ends at the edge of Lincoln Park.

Tracy Baim

Grand Marshal. Founder of Windy City Times. Decades documenting LGBTQ+ Chicago.

Mona Noriega

Grand Marshal. Opened Lambda Legal Midwest. On the board of Equality Illinois.

Evette Cardona

Grand Marshal. Long-time activist alongside Mona Noriega.

Alliance of Illinois Judges

Grand Marshal. Led by Judge Jill Rose Quinn, Illinois's first openly trans judge and elected official.

For the boys: bring water, hats, sunscreen, and your loudest cheer. For everyone: bring the heart.
