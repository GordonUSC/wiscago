How the Team Trophy works

There is no "winner" and no "loser" here. Every point any one of you earns is added to one shared trophy for all three of you. Help your brother get a hard one right. Read a question out loud so everyone can think. The faster you work as a team, the higher the trophy climbs. Reach 500 and you are officially Wiscago Champions.
