Sunday Pride Day

The biggest parade.

On Sunday, you will see colors you have never seen all in one place. The rainbow flag everywhere. Music. Floats. People dancing. The parade goes for two miles through Chicago's Northalsted neighborhood. The whole city says one big thing: everyone gets to be exactly who they are.

A note for the boys

The theme this year is "Free to Be Proud." That means: be proud of who you are, and be proud of everyone else's who-they-are too. The parade is for everyone. Including you. Welcome.
